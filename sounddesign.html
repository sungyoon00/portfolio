Sound Design for Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

Sound Design for Arcane Season 2.

Sound Design practice for dialogue localisation using two distinct reverbs for different locatinos. Reverberant energy level was kept constant, while direct energy level was manipulated, imitate real world acoustics. Automation of EQ to simulate the natural absorption of high frequencies by air.
